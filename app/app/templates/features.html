Features

Discover how the RemindMe revolutionizes reminder management through advanced OCR technology.

Advanced OCR

Optical character recognition with high accuracy to extract information from documents, images, and scanned texts.

Smart Reminders

Automated system that schedules and sends reminders based on information extracted by OCR from your documents.

High Performance

Developed in Django for maximum efficiency and speed in processing documents and scheduling tasks.

Multiple Formats

Support for various file formats including PDF, PNG, JPG, and scanned documents.

Guaranteed Security

Local processing of documents ensures complete privacy and security of your personal and professional data.

Simple Interface

Intuitive command-line interface that is easy to use, ideal for integration into existing workflows.
